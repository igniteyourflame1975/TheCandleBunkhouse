Our Story

From Hobby to Heart & Soul

Hi, I'm Raquel — your Candle Lady at The Candle Bunk House. What started as a love of fragrances became something so much greater: a small business built on passion, perseverance, and the belief of my family.

My journey wasn't without challenges — finding the right wick, perfecting fragrance ratios, eliminating tunneling. Through trial and error, I found my craft. Today, every candle I pour carries that story.

My husband John and I are both left-handed Arizonans who love travel, dachshunds, and — of course — beautiful scents. We're proud to bring the warmth of the Southwest to your home.

"Wherever life plants you, bloom with grace."

Why We're Different

Allergy-Conscious by Design

One customer said it best: "The only candles and wax melts that don't give me a headache." That's the standard we hold ourselves to every single time.

🌿
Parasoy Wax Blend
Our parasoy blend burns cleaner than paraffin alone — minimal soot and a pure, even scent throw that's gentler on your airways.
🚫
Phthalate-Free Fragrances
Every fragrance oil we use is phthalate-free. No hormone disruptors, no harsh chemicals. Just pure, beautiful scent you can breathe in with confidence.
🕯️
Cotton Wicks Only
Lead-free cotton wicks mean a clean, even burn with no toxic heavy metal emissions. Better for you, your home, and the air you breathe.
🧪
Perfected Ratios
Raquel spent years perfecting fragrance ratios — enough scent to fill a room beautifully, but never overwhelming or headache-inducing.
🏡
Home & Family Safe
Made without harsh additives, our candles are suitable for homes with children, pets, and scent-sensitive individuals. Burn with peace of mind.
✨
Long Clean Burn
Zero ash build-up, no tunneling. Our candles are engineered to look great, burn evenly, and last up to 45+ hours.
Made in Arizona

Inspired by the Desert & Its Sunsets

Saint David, Arizona isn't just where we make our candles — it's our muse. The wide-open skies, dramatic saguaro landscapes, and sunsets that paint the horizon in gold and orange every single evening.

Every scent we create carries a piece of that Arizona spirit — from our Desert Citrus that captures a warm desert morning to the earthy Fog & Fern that bottles the magic of monsoon rain on dry earth.
